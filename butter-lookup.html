BUTTER LOOKUP

REFERENCE WEIGHTS FOR COMMON VOLUMES

Quick reference for converting butter volumes to grams. Values are based on a standard U.S. stick of butter and typical baking references so you can move between cups, spoons, and grams without guessing.

Ingredient
Unsalted or salted butter
Volume Grams
1 cup 227 g
¾ cup 170 g
½ cup (1 stick) 113 g
⅓ cup 75.7 g
¼ cup 56.8 g
1 tablespoon 14.2 g
1 teaspoon 4.7 g

Weights assume a standard U.S. stick of butter (4 oz / 113 g) and level volume measurements. Small differences between brands and fat content are normal.

Ingredient weights vary naturally by brand, moisture content, and measurement technique. Kokori lookup values represent typical ingredient weights reported across standard baking references and internal measurements. They are provided for informational use only.
